Change Destiny

Brand
SK-II
Type
Brand Platform
Year
2013
Audience
Women 25-44

Project Overview

Women around the world, especially in Asia, face immense societal expectations and pressures about beauty standards, age, and how to live their lives. Many women feel destined to follow a predetermined path—and as a brand that supports women in defying skin aging, we wanted to send a bold message that goes beyond skincare.

Together with SK-II's ambassadors and influential local female voices, we challenged harmful stereotypes by empowering women to define success on their own terms. The campaign resonated deeply with our target audience, establishing a brand platform that has endured for over 10 years and influenced award-winning work like the Marriage Market documentary (→) through its powerful message.

Change Destiny became more than a campaign—it evolved into a movement that encouraged women worldwide to take control of their narrative and redefine what it means to live life on their own terms.

Campaign Assets

Tang Wei, actor and SK-II's main ambassador, delivered the message that the only person you are destined to become is the person you decide to be.
The campaign was supported by surrounding stories of societal pressure that women face across professions and empowerment in redefining what it means to be successful.
