There's something quietly satisfying about carrying this wallet. It's not trying to impress anyone - no logos, no flashy details, just thoughtful engineering made tangible. The leather molds to your cards over time, creating this perfect clam-like fit that feels custom-made. I love how it pairs with my iPhone - the rounded corners align perfectly, making them feel like they were designed together. Veilance removed everything unnecessary, even the stitches, leaving only what serves a purpose. It's the kind of object that makes you appreciate good design every time you reach for it.
