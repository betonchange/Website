I started my career at P&G learning the fundamentals of marketing-led business building. I've spent the last 9 years at Meta touching all aspects of marketing - business, product, policy and brand - working on Facebook App, Meta, and currently a Brand Lead at Instagram.

I've been lucky to work on brands going through transformational phases : navigating a brand crisis [1], expanding the company vision [2], regaining relevance with youth audiences [3], and communicating social impact to policymakers [4]. Through it all, I've learned that everything starts with understanding the audience deeply and telling the story from their perspective.

I'm a designer at heart. I have a deep appreciation for thoughtfully designed products [5], with the ambition to leave one behind one day. I believe that ultimately, a great product makes the brand.

I'm passionate about ensuring technology creates positive change. I choose to building and fixing problems from within, and telling stories that inspire more good.
